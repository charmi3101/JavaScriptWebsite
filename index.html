Welcome To Fashion Store!

The Fashion store was launched in 2019. Beginning with the idea of creating the perfect t-shirt and dress for women, TFS has since emerged into producing full, seasonal collections. Offering everything from your favorite tees to leather jackets and cashmere sweaters, the collection is always motivated by life on the west coast.TFS has a very faithful following of customers. TFS is based in Toronto where Lauren and April continue to keep perfecting the perfect tee and the clothes you want to live in.
